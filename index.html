    About Me

    I am an expert in software performance and SIMD programming.

    On my website I want to share with you some of the interesting and challenging problems I have worked on as well as write about topics that I hope you’ll find interesting.

    I'm a software engineer at Google working on making TensorFlow Lite as fast as possible on phones, browsers and servers.

    I previously worked at Prophesee where it was my job to process the data from their range of innovative event based cameras in real-time on both embedded systems and PC.

    In my previous role as research and development director at Numscale, I developed real-time computer vision libraries on x86 and Arm, including Fast Fourrier Transforms (FFT) which in many cases were faster than Intel’s MKL and the FFTW.
